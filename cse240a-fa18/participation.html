Course Participation

By 9pm on the night before the class, everyone must submit one brief question or comment about each of the assigned readings. These will be submitted on on https://tritoned.ucsd.edu/.

The comments/questions can be of the following form but you are not limited to this list:

  • Possible new ideas how to extend the paper
  • Critical questions about the methodologies and conclusions in the paper
  • Comments about the relation of the reading to previous readings
  • Why the paper is important and why it should be cited
  • Summary of the most important things you learned from the paper
  • The main insights from the paper

Furthermore, you must actively participate in class discussions for papers that you do not present. You must say something about every paper we discuss! Attendance is mandatory unless you get explicit permission from the instructor to be absent.
